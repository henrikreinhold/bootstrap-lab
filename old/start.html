What's new?

 

Log in with your Google Apps account

If your Jayway email is set up correctly on your profile, you should be able to log in with your mail account credentials.

Team skill goals

Teams can now decide what the goal levels should be for various skills, and you can see how the team lives up to those levels on each team's page. Click Skill Goals and Edit to change the goals for a team.

About logging in

KPerson uses the Jayway Active Directory to authenticate users, and does not store your password. Please go through the normal support channels if you can't log in to KPerson - your password may have expired in Active Directory. Contact admin@jayway.com for more help.

You can also log in using your Google Apps account, this works independently of the Active Directory.
